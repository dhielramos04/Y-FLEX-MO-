Meet the Researcher

Developed for URS Learners

Y-FLEX MO is a digital learning material created by Dianna L. Ramos, a master's student actively teaching and conducting research at the University of Rizal System - Morong Campus (URS).

This platform was developed by the researcher as part of the study titled "Gamified Yoga Instructions: An E‑Guide". The project aims to provide an engaging and interactive digital learning material for Physical Education students. As the creator of Y - Flex Mo, the author designed this web‑based gamified e‑guide to help learners enjoy yoga lessons through an accessible, motivating, and technology‑enhanced environment. The material integrates clear instructions, visual demonstrations, and level‑based activities that support student participation and holistic development.
